About Me

When I was little, the first thing I would do after completing my schoolwork was hop on my dads computer and play some videogames like:

  • Tonka Construction
  • Lego Island
  • Baldurs Gate II (okay maybe i'm joking with this one)

These games were the beginning of my amazing journey with computers. In high-school, I attended a technical school for media communications. It was at this point I was introduced into the world of web-design. My teacher Kevin kickstarted and pushed me to delve deeper into the programming aspect of things, and this is where I am now. I dabbled with multiple languages and tools along the way. In order, these were some things I tried:

  1. C# with:
    • Unity Game Engine
    • ASP.NET
    • Visual Studio Development
  2. C/C++ with:
    • CLion by JetBrains
    • Unreal Engine
    • Visual Studio Development

Equipped with the knowledge i gained along the way, I plan on diving deep into web-development, and furthering my education down the road by attending West Virginia University (WVU) for Engineering. I want to collaborate with others on both large and small scale projects. As the saying goes, Teamwork Makes The Dream Work!

Also, If you couldn't tell from above, I enjoy my fair bit of gaming. Game-Development is a huge interest for me, and could go on for hours talking about and brainstorming game ideas. I've expiremented with the Unity game engine for a couple years, and have thoroughly enjoyed every second working with it. Whenever I'm designing things, whether it be websites or just learning something new about development, you will always find a smile on my face. It will never fail!
